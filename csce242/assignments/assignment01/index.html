How To Create A Character

When creating a character, it is essential to focus first on what kind of character you want to create. Do you want someone who is brutish and blunt, or would you prefer someone calm and reserved? What kind of skills do you want your character to have? These are important questions when you're considering making a character.

It's also essential to consider the statistics of a character. When playing role-playing games, stats are essential to overall gameplay-- these are also impacted by species and class! There are six statistics to consider in Dungeons & Dragons, specifically: Strength, Charisma, Constitution, Dexterity, Wisdom, & Intelligence. These are cornerstones for what abilities your character will have.

Reminder: What should you focus on?

  • Species & Class
  • Statistics (aka Ability Scores)
  • Character/Personality Traits
